About
MeraPulse

An end-to-end machine learning project built to predict heart disease risk from clinical parameters.

01

The Problem

Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death worldwide. Early risk identification using patient data can support preventive care and timely medical intervention.

02

The Approach

We trained an ensemble of Logistic Regression and Random Forest classifiers on the Cleveland Heart Disease dataset (303 patients, 13 clinical features) using soft voting to combine model outputs.

03

The Stack

Built with Python, scikit-learn, Flask, and deployed on Render. The model trains on startup — no separate setup required.

Dataset

Cleveland Heart Disease Dataset — 303 patient records, 13 clinical features, binary classification target (presence or absence of heart disease).
